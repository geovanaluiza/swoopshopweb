Campus Life

A 56-acre classroom you happen to live on.

Kirkland is a quiet lakeside town ten minutes from downtown Seattle. The campus sits under evergreens, with a creek running through the middle of it. There's a chapel at the heart of it all — and a record label, a nursing sim lab, and a Grammy-winning studio.

Worship that means something

Weekly chapel. Student-led bands. Prayer groups that actually feel like family.

Mentors, not lecture halls
